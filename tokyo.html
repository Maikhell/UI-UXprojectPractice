Fun Facts

"Tokyo was originally known as Edo, which means 'estuary' in Japanese. It became the capital of Japan in 1868 when Emperor Meiji moved his seat from Kyoto to Edo, renaming it Tokyo, which means 'Eastern Capital.'"
